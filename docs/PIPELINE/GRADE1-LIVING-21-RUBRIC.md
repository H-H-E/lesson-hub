# Grade 1 Science: Living Things — Assessment Rubric

## Criteria & Performance Levels

| Criterion | 4 — Excellent | 3 — Proficient | 2 — Developing | 1 — Beginning |
|-----------|---------------|----------------|----------------|---------------|
| **1. Identifying Living Things** | Correctly identifies living vs. non-living things with detailed reasons (e.g., "Plants are living because they grow and need water") | Identifies living things with simple reasons (e.g., "Animals are living because they move") | Identifies some living things but may confuse non-living with living | Unable to distinguish living from non-living things |
| **2. Describing Characteristics of Living Things** | Describes 3+ characteristics (growth, movement, respiration, reproduction) accurately | Describes 2 characteristics accurately | Describes 1-2 characteristics with some errors | Cannot describe characteristics of living things |
| **3. Sorting & Classifying Living Things** | Sorts living things into multiple categories (e.g., plants/animals, habitat, diet) with explanations | Sorts living things into 2 clear categories | Sorts with some errors or needs guidance | Cannot sort or classify living things |
| **4. Understanding Life Cycles** | Explains complete life cycle of a plant or animal in order (seed → plant/animal → adult → reproduction) | Describes main stages in order | Names 1-2 stages but order may be mixed | Cannot describe or name stages |

---

**Scoring:** Total / 16 points

- **13–16:** Exceeds expectations
- **9–12:** Meets expectations
- **5–8:** Approaching expectations
- **1–4:** Needs support
