## BC Curriculum Standards (British Columbia)

### Big Ideas (Grade 1 Science)
**Living things have features and behaviours that help them survive in their environment.**

#### Sample Inquiry Questions:
- How do local plants and animals depend on their environment?
- How do plants and animals use their features to respond to stimuli?
- How do plants and animals adapt when their basic needs are not being met?

## Cross-Curricular Integration Notes

### Indigenous Perspectives (First Peoples)
- Integrate Indigenous classification systems alongside scientific classification
- Include local Indigenous names for plants and animals
- Connect seasonal rounds and traditional land use to animal behaviours
- Invite Elder/knowledge keeper to share traditional ecological knowledge

### Environmental Connections
- Consider environmental consequences of human actions on living things
- Develop stewardship habits for caring for living things in classroom/school
- Connect to local ecosystems and native species
