## 📝 Teacher Notes

- **Format options:** These prompts work as discussion starters, draw-and-write activities, or digital responses with voice recording.
- **Scaffolding:** For students needing support, provide word banks or sentence starters.
- **Timing:** Use 1-2 prompts per session or select based on lesson focus.
- **Differentiation:** Advanced students can write full sentences; others can draw or use dictated responses.
- **Portfolio:** Collect reflections to show growth over the unit.

---

*Reflection helps students own their learning and see themselves as curious scientists!*
