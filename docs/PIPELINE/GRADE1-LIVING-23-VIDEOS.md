# Grade 1 Science: Living Things - Recommended Videos

## Overview
This curated list contains 4 educational videos ideal for introducing Grade 1 students to the "Living Things" science unit. All videos are free, age-appropriate, and cover key concepts including:
- What makes something "living" vs "non-living"
- Characteristics of living things (needs, growth, reproduction)
- Plants and animals as living things

---
