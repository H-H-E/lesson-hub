# Grade 1 Science - Living Things Vocabulary

## Core Vocabulary List

| Word | Child-Friendly Definition |
|------|---------------------------|
| **Living Things** | Things that are alive. They can grow, move, and need food and water. |
| **Animal** | A living thing that can move around and needs food to eat. Dogs, cats, birds, and fish are all animals. |
| **Plant** | A living thing that grows in the ground. Plants need sunlight and water to live. Trees, flowers, and grass are plants. |
| **Grow** | To get bigger over time. Plants grow from seeds. Animals grow from babies to adults. |
| **Food** | Something living things eat to get energy. Animals eat food. Plants make their own food using sunlight. |
| **Water** | A clear liquid that living things need to survive. Plants need water to grow. Animals need water to drink. |
| **Sunlight** | The bright light that comes from the sun. Plants use sunlight to make food. |
| **Air** | The invisible gas all around us that we breathe. Living things need air to live. |
| **Seed** | A tiny part of a plant that can grow into a new plant. Seeds are like babies for plants. |
| **Leaf** | The green flat part of a plant that catches sunlight. Leaves help plants make food. |
| **Root** | The part of a plant that grows underground. Roots hold the plant in place and soak up water. |
| **Stem** | The skinny part of a plant that holds it up. Water travels through the stem to the rest of the plant. |
| **Flower** | The pretty part of a plant that makes seeds. Flowers have colors that attract bees and butterflies. |
| **Baby** | A young animal or plant. A puppy is a baby dog. A seedling is a baby plant. |
| **Adult** | A fully grown animal or plant. Adult animals can have babies of their own. |
| **Habitat** | The natural home where an animal or plant lives. A forest is a habitat. A pond is a habitat too. |
| **Needs** | Things living things must have to stay alive. All living things need food, water, air, and a home. |
| **Move** | To go from one place to another. Animals can move by walking, flying, swimming, or crawling. |
