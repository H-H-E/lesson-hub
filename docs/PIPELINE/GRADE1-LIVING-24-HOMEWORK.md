# Grade 1 Science: Living Things - Homework Assignment

## Overview

**Topic:** Living Things  
**Grade Level:** 1  
**Duration:** 1 week (to be completed at home with family)  
**Format:** Hands-on activities and simple written/drawn responses

---

## Weekly Homework Tasks

### 🌱 Day 1: Living Things Hunt

**Task:** Go on a nature walk around your home, yard, or a nearby park. Look for living things!

**Instructions:**
- Find and draw 3 living things you see (animals, insects, birds, or plants)
- Ask a grown-up to help you write the name of each one

**My Living Things:**
1. Draw: ____________
2. Draw: ____________
3. Draw: ____________

---

### 🐛 Day 2: How Living Things Grow

**Task:** Choose one living thing and draw how it changes over time.

**Think about:**
- A baby animal growing into an adult
- A seed growing into a plant
- You growing taller!

**Draw your living thing at two stages:**

```
Young Stage:          Grown-Up Stage:
[Draw here]           [Draw here]
```

## Family Connection

### 🌿 Nature Scavenger Hunt (Optional Family Activity)

Go outside together and find:
- 🌸 1 flower
- 🐛 1 bug
- 🌳 1 tree or plant
- 🐦 1 bird or animal

Talk about how each one is living!

---

## Materials Needed

- Pencil and crayons
- This worksheet
- Access to outdoor space for nature walks

---

## Teacher Notes

- Activities are designed for independent completion with minimal adult support
- Encourage creativity in drawings
- Accept any reasonable answers demonstrating understanding of living vs. non-living
- Extension: Students can bring in a photo of their favorite living thing from home
