# Careers Connected to Grade 1 Science: Living Things

Grade 1 students explore living things—plants, animals, and humans. Here are **5-6 real-world careers** that work with living things:

## 1. Veterinarian (Vet)
Doctors who take care of sick or injured animals. Vets help pets like dogs, cats, and rabbits stay healthy. They check animals, give shots, and fix broken bones.

**What they do:** Examine animals, give medicine, perform surgery

## 2. Farmer
People who grow food and raise animals. Farmers plant seeds, water crops, and collect eggs or milk. They take care of living things every day!

**What they do:** Plant crops, feed animals, harvest food

## 3. Zookeeper
Workers who care for animals at the zoo. They feed the animals, clean their homes, and make sure the animals are happy and healthy.

**What they do:** Feed animals, clean enclosures, watch animal behavior

## 4. Botanist (Plant Scientist)
Scientists who study plants. They learn how plants grow, what they need to survive, and how to help them stay healthy.

**What they do:** Study plants, conduct experiments, grow new plants

## 5. gardener/ Landscaper
People who design and care for gardens. They plant flowers, trim trees, and make parks and yards beautiful.

**What they do:** Plant flowers, mow lawns, design outdoor spaces

## 6. Pet Store Worker
Store employees who help care for and sell pets and pet supplies. They feed the animals, clean their cages, and help customers find the right pet.

**What they do:** Care for store animals, help customers, stock supplies

---

**Why these careers matter:** Each of these jobs helps students see that caring for living things is important and can be a real job!

*Aligned with Grade 1 Science: Living Things (plants, animals, needs of living things)*
