# Digital Citizenship

## Online Safety

### For Students
- Keep personal information private
- Ask a grown-up before going online
- Be kind to others online

### For Parents
- Supervise online activity
- Set up parental controls
- Talk about internet safety

---

## Media Literacy

### Evaluating Sources
- Who made this?
- Is it true?
- Why was it made?
