## Unit 1: Kitchen Safety

### Safety Rules

**Before Cooking:**

- [ ] Wash hands for 20 seconds
- [ ] Tie back long hair
- [ ] Wear closed-toe shoes
- [ ] Read the entire recipe
- [ ] Gather all ingredients
- [ ] Get out needed equipment

**During Cooking:**

- [ ] Stay in the kitchen
- [ ] Use pot holders
- [ ] Turn handles inward
- [ ] Keep workspace clean
- [ ] Ask for help when needed
- [ ] Never taste raw ingredients

**After Cooking:**

- [ ] Clean up workspace
- [ ] Put away ingredients
- [ ] Wash all dishes

---

### Emergency Procedures

**If a fire starts:**
1. Turn off heat
2. Cover with lid (not water!)
3. Get adult help

**If you get burned:**
1. Run cool water over burn
2. Tell an adult
3. Get first aid

## Unit 4: Simple Recipes

### Recipe 1: Peanut Butter Banana Wrap

**Ingredients:**
- 1 whole wheat tortilla
- 2 tbsp peanut butter
- 1 banana
- 1 tbsp honey (optional)

**Steps:**
1. Spread peanut butter on tortilla
2. Place banana at one end
3. Drizzle with honey
4. Roll up tightly
5. Cut in half diagonally

### Recipe 2: Fruit Salad

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup strawberries
- 1 banana
- 1/2 cup blueberries
- 1/2 cup grapes
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

**Steps:**
1. Wash all fruit
2. Cut strawberries in half
3. Slice banana
4. Combine all fruit in bowl
5. Add lemon juice and mix gently
6. Chill and serve
