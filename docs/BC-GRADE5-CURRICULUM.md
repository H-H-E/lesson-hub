# Grade 5 Curriculum - Code-First Inquiry

## BC Grade 5 Standards

### Science Big Ideas
- All living things have complex systems
- Matter has measurable physical and chemical properties
- Energy flows through ecosystems
- Earth materials cycle through the environment

### ADST
- Design requires research and planning
- Making uses tools, materials, and processes
- Technologies are developed by people for purposes

---

## Module 1: Body Systems 🫀

**Duration:** 45 minutes

### Learning Objectives
1. Identify major body systems
2. Explain how systems work together
3. Model a body system

---

## Module 2: Properties of Matter 🧪

**Duration:** 45 minutes

### Learning Objectives
1. Test physical properties of materials
2. Describe chemical properties
3. Conduct a fair test

---

## Module 3: Ecosystems and Energy 🌲

**Duration:** 45 minutes

### Learning Objectives
1. Show how energy flows through food chains
2. Create a food web
3. Explain ecosystem balance

---

## Module 4: The Rock Cycle 🪨

**Duration:** 45 minutes

### Learning Objectives
1. Identify types of rocks
2. Explain the rock cycle
3. Connect to local geology

### First Peoples Integration
- Traditional uses of rocks
- Indigenous geology knowledge
- Local sacred sites

---
