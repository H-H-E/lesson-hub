# Grade 6 Curriculum - Code-First Inquiry

## BC Grade 6 Standards

### Science Big Ideas
- Multicellular organisms have organ systems
- Everyday materials can be transformed
- Energy is conserved and can be transferred
- Earth's crust has changing features

### ADST
- Design processes involve multiple stages
- Skills are refined through making
- Technologies have constraints

---

## Module 1: Cell Systems 🧫

### Learning Objectives
1. Describe cell organization
2. Identify major organ systems
3. Explain how systems work together

---

## Module 2: Chemical Changes ⚗️

### Learning Objectives
1. Identify signs of chemical change
2. Conduct safe experiments
3. Explain conservation of mass

---

## Module 3: Energy Transfer 🔌

### Learning Objectives
1. Trace energy through systems
2. Design efficient energy use
3. Explain conservation

---

## Module 4: Earth's Features 🏔️

### Learning Objectives
1. Identify geological features
2. Explain plate tectonics
3. Connect to local geology

---

## Module 5: Digital Citizenship 💻

### Learning Objectives
1. Be safe online
2. Evaluate sources
3. Create digital content ethically

---
