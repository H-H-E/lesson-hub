# The Lazy Farmer

Once upon a time, there was a farmer named Tom. Tom had a big farm with cows, chickens, and many acres of corn. But Tom was very lazy. He did not like to work hard.

Every morning, Tom would sleep until noon. While other farmers were already planting their seeds, Tom was still in bed. "I'll do it tomorrow," he would say.

One day, an old woman came to visit Tom's farm. She looked at his overgrown fields and asked, "Why is your farm so messy?"

"I'm tired," Tom replied. "I'll fix it tomorrow."

The old woman shook her head. "Tomorrow is not a day, Tom. It is an excuse. If you want a good harvest, you must work today."

Tom thought about what the woman said. He realized she was right. From that day on, Tom woke up with the sun. He planted seeds, fed his animals, and fixed his fences.

By autumn, Tom had the best harvest in the whole county. He won a blue ribbon at the fair! Everyone asked Tom his secret.

"I learned that today is the only day that matters," Tom smiled. "Tomorrow is too late, and yesterday is gone."
