# Grade 2 Science: Plant Life Cycle - Interactive Simulation

## Teacher Guide

### Learning Objectives
- Students will understand the stages of a plant's life cycle
- Students will sequence the stages in correct order
- Students will identify what plants need to grow

### Materials Needed
- Printed plant cards (provided below)
- Yarn or string
- Large poster board
- Glue sticks
- Seed packets for extension activity

---

## Simulation Activity: "Plant Life Cycle Dance"

### Warm-Up (5 minutes)
Ask students: "Has anyone watched a seed grow into a plant? What did you see?"

Show the class the illustrated poster of the plant life cycle.

### Activity Instructions (20 minutes)

**Step 1: Model the Cycle**
1. Give each student a card showing a plant stage:
   - 🌱 Seed
   - 🌿 Sprout/Seedling  
   - 🌸 Flower/Plant
   - 🍎 Fruit with Seeds

**Step 2: Arrange in Order**
2. Have students arrange themselves in a circle showing the correct sequence:
   ```
   Seed → Sprout → Plant → Flower → Fruit → Seed (cycle repeats!)
   ```

**Step 3: Act It Out**
3. Students act out each stage while you narrate:
   - "The seed sleeps in the soil..." (crouch down)
   - "Rain comes and the seed wakes up..." (stand slowly)
   - "The sprout reaches for the sun..." (reach arms up)
   - "Flowers bloom and bees visit..." (wave arms like butterflies)
   - "Fruit grows with new seeds inside..." (hold belly)

### Discussion Questions
1. What does a seed need to start growing? (water, soil, sun)
2. Why is the cycle important? (plants keep reproducing)
3. What would happen if there were no flowers?

### Extension Activities
- **Science Journal**: Draw each stage of a bean plant's growth over 2 weeks
- **Garden Visit**: Plant seeds in cups to observe at home
- **Matching Game**: Pair plant parts with their functions

### Assessment
- Can students sequence all 5 stages correctly?
- Can they explain what plants need at each stage?
- Can they identify seeds in different fruits?

## Student Card Templates (Print and Cut)

| Stage | Illustration | Description |
|-------|--------------|-------------|
| Seed | 🌱 | A tiny package with a baby plant inside |
| Sprout | 🌿 | A small green shoot coming out of the ground |
| Plant | 🌿 | A growing stem with leaves |
| Flower | 🌸 | Beautiful petals that make seeds |
| Fruit | 🍎 | Contains seeds for new plants |

---

## Differentiation

**For struggling learners:**
- Provide sequence strips they can arrange
- Use real objects (seeds, small plants) as tactile aids

**For advanced learners:**
- Research different types of seeds
- Create a more detailed diagram showing what happens inside each stage
