# Grade 4 Science - Biodiversity
## Day 1: Living Things in Our World

**Duration:** 45 minutes

---

## Learning Objectives

1. **Define** biodiversity
2. **Categorize** local living things
3. **Explain** why biodiversity matters

---

## Materials

- [ ] Nature walk supplies (magnifiers, bags)
- [ ] Classification posters
- [ ] BC wildlife book/pictures
- [ ] Sorting cards

---

## Opening (5 min)

**Hook:**
Show images of: Rainforest 🦜🦎🐒 vs Arctic 🐧❄️🐻‍❄️

"What's different?"

"Biodiversity! Different places have different living things!"

---

## Exploration (15 min)

**Activity: Biodiversity Walk**

Go outside!

Find and collect (with eyes only):
- Plants
- Animals (or signs of animals)
- Insects
- Fungi

**Record findings**

---

## Discovery (10 min)

**Back in class:**

Sort findings:

| Category | What We Found |
|----------|---------------|
| Plants | |
| Animals | |
| Insects | |
| Other | |

**Key idea:**
Biodiversity = ALL the different kinds of living things in an area

---

## Create (10 min)

**Poster:**

Groups create "Our School's Biodiversity" poster

- Draw what we found
- Add labels
- Title

---

## Check (5 min)

Exit ticket: "What is biodiversity in your own words?"

---

## Assessment

- Participation in walk
- Classification accuracy
- Understanding
