# Learning Centers Setup - Grade 1

## Science Centers

### Center 1: Living Things
- Sorting cards (living/non-living)
- Magnifying glasses
- Nature items collection
- Picture books about animals

### Center 2: Matter
- Ice cubes (watch them melt)
- Water containers
- Different materials to touch
- Sand/soil samples

### Center 3: Light & Shadow
- Flashlights
- White paper
- Various objects for shadows
- Shadow puppet ideas

---

## Math Centers

### Center 1: Counting
- Number cards
- Counting objects (beans, blocks)
- Number puzzles
- Dice games

### Center 2: Shapes
- Shape blocks
- Geoboards
- Shape hunts
- Building materials

---

## Management

### Rotation
- 15-20 minutes per center
- Signal for rotation
- Clean-up song

### Tips
- Label everything
- Student jobs
- Choice boards
