# Grade 1 Science - Living Things
## Week 1 Day 2: What Do Living Things Need?

**Duration:** 45 minutes

---

## Learning Objectives

1. **Identify** what living things need to survive (food, water, air, home)
2. **Explain** why each need is important
3. **Sort** examples by what living things need

---

## Materials

- [ ] "Needs" chart (4 sections)
- [ ] Picture cards of animals/plants
- [ ] Student journals
- [ ] Crayons

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Opening (5 min)

**Review:**
"What does it mean to be ALIVE?"

"Let's remember: Living things MOVE, GROW, and NEED things."

**Hook:**
"Today we're going to find out: What do ALL living things need?"

---

### Exploration (15 min)

**Activity: Needs Sort**

In groups, students sort picture cards:

**Needs Categories:**
1. Food (apple, seeds, bugs)
2. Water (rain, river, bowl)
3. Air (wind, breathing)
4. Home (nest, burrow, tree)

**Groups report out:**
- "Animals need _____"
- "Plants need _____"

---

### Discovery (10 min)

**Create "Living Things Need" Chart**

Together, make class chart:

| Need | Animals | Plants |
|------|---------|--------|
| Food | 🐕🐱🐟 | 🌱🌻🌲 |
| Water | 💧🐠 | 💧🌿 |
| Air | 💨🐦 | 💨🍃 |
| Home | 🏠🪺 | 🌳🪨 |

---

### Create (10 min)

**Draw in journals:**
"Draw a picture of a living thing and show what it needs"

Include:
- 1 thing it needs
- Label or tell what it is

---

### Check (5 min)

Share drawings with partner
"Tell your partner: This is a ____ and it needs ____"

---

## Differentiation

**Level 1:** Pre-sorted examples, teacher-guided
**Level 2:** Partner work, some guidance
**Level 3:** Add "sun" - does it need anything?

---

## Assessment

- Observation during sort
- Journal drawings
- Partner share
