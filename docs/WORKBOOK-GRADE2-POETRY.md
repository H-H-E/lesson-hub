## What is Poetry?

Poetry is writing that uses imagination and feelings. Poems can be short and fun!

---

## Types of Poems

### 1. Haiku (3 lines)

- Line 1: 5 syllables
- Line 2: 7 syllables  
- Line 3: 5 syllables

**Example:**

```
The sun is very hot (5)
The flowers drink the sunshine (7)
They grow tall and bright (5)
```

**Your Turn:**

```

```

### 2. Acrostic Poem

Use the letters of a word to start each line.

**Example:**

```
S  hining in the sky
U  p above so bright
N  ew day has begun
```

**Your Turn:**

Choose a word and write:

```
M
Y
W
O
R
D
```

---

### 3. Shape Poem

The words make a shape!

**Example:**

```
  ⭐ ⭐ ⭐
 ⭐ ⭐ ⭐ ⭐ ⭐
 ⭐ ⭐ ⭐ ⭐ ⭐
  ⭐ ⭐ ⭐
```

**Your Turn:**

Draw a shape and write words inside!

[Draw your shape here]

_____________________

---

### 4. Alliteration Poem

Same sound at the beginning of nearby words.

**Example:**

```
Big blue butterflies
Buzzing by the bushes
```
