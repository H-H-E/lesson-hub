# Questioning Strategies

## Wait Time
- Wait 3-5 seconds
- Allow thinking time

## Question Types

### Closed
One answer
- What's 2+2?

### Open
Multiple answers
- What do you notice?

### Probing
Follow-up
- Can you tell me more?

---

## Prompts

- "Tell me what you know about..."
- "What makes you say that?"
- "How did you figure that out?"
- "Does anyone have a different idea?"
