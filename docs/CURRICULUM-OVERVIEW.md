# Code-First Inquiry Curriculum
## BC Science K-5 Overview

This curriculum integrates computational thinking with BC Science standards using a Code-First Inquiry approach.

---

## Grade-by-Grade Summary

| Grade | Modules | Focus |
|-------|---------|-------|
| K | 6 | My World, Senses, Weather, Materials, Animals, Patterns |
| 1 | 6 | Living Things, Matter, Light/Shadow, Sky Patterns, Sound, Sorting |
| 2 | 6 | Life Cycles, Properties, Light/Sound, Earth Materials, Plants, Animals |
| 3 | 4 | Biodiversity, Forces, Energy, Soil/Ecosystems |
| 4 | 5 | Senses, Matter, Energy Transfer, Earth's Surface, Habitats |
| 5 | 4 | Body Systems, Matter Properties, Ecosystems, Rock Cycle |

---

## Approach

Each module uses the Code Lab format:
1. Hook - Mystery demo
2. Exploration - Interactive simulation
3. Discovery - Share findings
4. Formalization - Science content
5. Create - Hands-on activity
6. Check - Exit ticket

---

## Standards Alignment

All modules align with:
- BC Science Curriculum (2016)
- ADST K-3 / 4-5
- First Peoples Principles of Learning

---

## Assessment

- Formative: Exit tickets, observations, discussions
- Summative: Projects, presentations
- Self-assessment: "I can" statements
