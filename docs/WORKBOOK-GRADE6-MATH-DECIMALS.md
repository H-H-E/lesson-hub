## Section 1: What Are Decimals?

Decimals are another way to show parts of a whole.

**Example:** 0.5 is the same as 1/2

### Place Value with Decimals

```
Hundreds | Tens | Ones | . | Tenths | Hundredths
   2       5      3   .     6        8
```

This number is 253.68

## Section 2: Comparing Decimals

### Remember: Line up the decimal points!

Compare: 3.45 and 3.54

```
3.45
3.54
```

- Compare ones: 3 = 3 ✓
- Compare tenths: 4 < 5

**So: 3.45 < 3.54**

## Section 3: Adding Decimals

**Step 1:** Line up the decimal points  
**Step 2:** Add zeros if needed  
**Step 3:** Add like normal  
**Step 4:** Bring the decimal down

**Example:**

```
   12.5
 +  4.32
 ------
  16.82
```

## Section 5: Converting Fractions to Decimals

### Method: Divide the numerator by the denominator

**Examples:**

1/2 = 1 ÷ 2 = **0.5**

3/4 = 3 ÷ 4 = **0.75**

1/4 = 1 ÷ 4 = **0.25**

3/10 = 0.3
