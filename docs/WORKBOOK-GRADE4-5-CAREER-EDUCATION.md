## Unit 3: Goal Setting

### Setting SMART Goals

- **S**pecific: What exactly will you do?
- **M**easurable: How will you know you did it?
- **A**chievable: Can you really do this?
- **R**elevant: Does it matter to you?
- **T**ime-bound: When will you finish?
