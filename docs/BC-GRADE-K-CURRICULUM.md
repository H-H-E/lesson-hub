# Kindergarten Curriculum - Code-First Inquiry

## BC Kindergarten Standards

### Science Big Ideas
- Daily and seasonal changes affect all living things
- Observable properties of materials

### ADST
- Curiosity and wonder lead to explorations
- People use technologies

---

## Module 1: My Senses 👀👂

**Duration:** 30 minutes

### Learning Objectives
1. Use all five senses to explore
2. Describe what we experience with each sense
3. Sort things by what sense we use

### The Mystery
> "What can you find that looks blue and sounds soft?"

### Activities
- Sense walk outside
- Sensory stations
- Sorting game

---

## Module 2: Weather Watchers ☀️🌧️

**Duration:** 30 minutes

### Learning Objectives
1. Observe the weather each day
2. Describe the weather
3. Notice patterns

### Activities
- Weather chart
- Weather journal
- Cloud watching

---

## Module 3: Things That Move 🚗

**Duration:** 30 minutes

### Learning Objectives
1. Find things that move
2. Describe how things move
3. Make something move

### Activities
- Movement walk
- Ramp experiments
- Simple push/pull toys

---

## Module 4: Living and Non-Living 🐕🌳

**Duration:** 30 minutes

### Learning Objectives
1. Tell if something is living or was never living
2. Describe what living things do
3. Find living things outside

### Activities
- Nature walk sorting
- Living things book
- What am I? game

---

## Module 5: Materials Matter 📦

**Duration:** 30 minutes

### Learning Objectives
1. Describe materials (hard, soft, bumpy, smooth)
2. Find different materials
3. Sort materials by properties

### Activities
- Sensory exploration
- Sorting by touch
- Texture collages

---

## Module 6: Patterns Everywhere 🔴🔵

**Duration:** 30 minutes

### Learning Objectives
1. See patterns in the world
2. Copy and continue patterns
3. Make our own patterns

### Activities
- Pattern hunt
- Bead patterns
- Pattern music

---

## Assessment

- Observation during activities
- Class discussions
- "Show me" demonstrations

---

*Kindergarten - Code-First Inquiry - BC Aligned*
