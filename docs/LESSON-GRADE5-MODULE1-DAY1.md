# Grade 5 Science - Body Systems
## Day 1: Your Body is Amazing!

**Duration:** 50 minutes

---

## Learning Objectives

1. **Identify** major body systems
2. **Explain** that cells work together to form systems
3. **Describe** what each system does

---

## Materials

- [ ] Human body poster/diagram
- [ ] Body systems sorting cards
- [ ] Video (optional): "How your body works"
- [ ] Journals

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Opening (5 min)

**Hook:**
"Your body is doing something right now that you're not even thinking about."

"BLINK your eyes. Your heart just beat. You just breathed."

"Today we're going to explore: How does your AMAZING body work?"

---

### Exploration (15 min)

**Video: "Journey through the Human Body"** (or teacher-led)

**As we watch, look for:**
- What parts are working together?
- What systems can you name?

---

### Discovery (15 min)

**Body Systems Introduction:**

**Together, create body systems chart:**

| System | What It Does | Parts |
|--------|--------------|-------|
| **Skeletal** | Holds us up, protects organs | Bones, skeleton |
| **Muscular** | Lets us move | Muscles |
| **Circulatory** | Moves blood | Heart, blood vessels |
| **Respiratory** | Breathing | Lungs, airways |
| **Digestive** | Breaks down food | Stomach, intestines |
| **Nervous** | Controls body | Brain, nerves |

---

### Create (10 min)

**Draw Your Body:**

In journals:
- Draw a person
- Label 3 systems you learned today
- Write one interesting fact

---

### Check (5 min)

Exit ticket: "Name 2 body systems and what they do"

---

## Differentiation

**Level 1:** Labeled diagram, word bank
**Level 2:** Basic labeling
**Level 3:** Research one system more

---

## Assessment

- Observation
- Journal
- Exit ticket
