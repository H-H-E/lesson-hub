# Grade 4 Curriculum - Code-First Inquiry

## BC Grade 4 Standards

### Science Big Ideas
- All living things sense and respond to their environment
- Matter has mass, takes up space, and has properties
- Energy can be transferred and transformed
- The surface of Earth changes

### ADST
- Design involves multiple processes
- Skills are developed through making
- Technologies are designed to solve problems

---

## Module 1: Senses and Responses 👀

**Duration:** 45 minutes

### Learning Objectives
1. Identify the 5 senses
2. Explain how animals respond to stimuli
3. Design an experiment testing a response

### The Mystery
> "How does a plant know which way to grow?"

### Code Exploration
- Simulation of plants growing toward light
- Student experiments with stimuli
- Discover: Plants respond too!

---

## Module 2: Properties of Matter ⚗️

**Duration:** 45 minutes

### Learning Objectives
1. Describe properties of matter
2. Test materials for properties
3. Explain how properties determine use

---

## Module 3: Energy Transfer 🔋

**Duration:** 45 minutes

### Learning Objectives
1. Show how energy moves from place to place
2. Create a simple system with energy transfer
3. Design an energy-efficient solution

---

## Module 4: Earth's Surface 🌍

**Duration:** 45 minutes

### Learning Objectives
1. Identify how Earth's surface changes
2. Describe natural processes that shape Earth
3. Investigate local geological features

### First Peoples Integration
- Local Indigenous knowledge of land
- Traditional land management practices
- Creation stories of local landscape

---

## Module 5: Habitats and Communities 🏠

**Duration:** 45 minutes

### Learning Objectives
1. Describe a habitat
2. Explain how animals adapt to habitats
3. Create a model habitat

---
