# Vocabulary Cards - Grade 1

## Module 1: Living Things

| Word | Definition | Example |
|------|------------|---------|
| living | Has needs, grows, changes | Dog, tree |
| non-living | Never was alive | Rock, book |
| survive | Stay alive | Food, water, air |

## Module 2: Matter

| Word | Definition | Example |
|------|------------|---------|
| solid | Keeps its shape | Rock, stick |
| liquid | Flows, takes shape | Water, juice |
| gas | Spreads out | Air, steam |
| melt | Solid becomes liquid | Ice to water |

## Module 3: Light & Shadow

| Word | Definition | Example |
|------|------------|---------|
| light | Lets us see | Sun, lamp |
| shadow | Dark shape when light blocked | Your silhouette |
| block | Stop light | Hand, book |

## Module 4: Sky Patterns

| Word | Definition | Example |
|------|------------|---------|
| pattern | Repeating thing | Moon phases |
| cycle | Thing that repeats | Day/night |
| rotate | Turn around | Earth spins |

## Module 5: Sound

| Word | Definition | Example |
|------|------------|---------|
| sound | What we hear | Music, voice |
| loud | Big sound | Thunder |
| quiet | Small sound | Whisper |
| vibrate | Fast movement | Guitar string |

## Module 6: Sorting

| Word | Definition | Example |
|------|------------|---------|
| sort | Put in groups | By color |
| classify | Sort by rules | Living/non-living |
| attribute | Quality to sort | Color, size |
