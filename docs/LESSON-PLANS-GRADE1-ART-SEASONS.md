# Grade 1 Art: Seasons and Weather - Art Lesson Collection

## Unit Overview

**Duration:** 4 lessons (40 min each)  
**Grade:** 1  
**Curriculum Area:** Visual Arts

### Learning Goals
- Express ideas through visual art
- Explore different art materials and techniques
- Connect art to seasons and weather

---

## Lesson 1: The Four Seasons Tree

### Materials
- Brown paper bag (torn into strips)
- White/blue paper background
- Tempera paint (green, brown, orange, red, yellow)
- Paintbrushes
- Glue

### Process

**Step 1 (10 min):** Discuss seasons. What does each season look like?

**Step 2 (15 min):** 
- Students paint 4 small squares in each season's colors
- Let dry

**Step 3 (15 min):**
- Create tree trunk from brown paper strips
- Glue seasonal squares in tree canopy

### Display
Hang trees in order showing the year cycle!

---

## Lesson 2: Weather Mobile

### Materials
- Paper plate
- String
- Markers, crayons
- Stickers/stamps
- Hole punch

### Process

**Step 1:** Decorate plate as sky for one weather type

**Step 2:** Add weather elements:
- ☀️ Sun
- 🌙 Moon
- ☁️ Clouds
- 🌧️ Rain drops
- ❄️ Snowflakes

**Step 3:** Punch hole, add string, hang!

### Options
- Create all four weather types
- Create favorite weather

---

## Lesson 3: Sun, Moon, and Stars

### Materials
- Black construction paper
- Yellow crayon/oil pastel
- White paint + toothbrush (for splatter stars)
- Glue
- Foil circles (moon)

### Process

**Step 1:** Create night sky background with paint splatter

**Step 2:** Use crayon to draw:
- Crescent moon
- Big and little stars

**Step 3:** Glue foil circle for full moon

---

## Lesson 4: Rainbow Wind Catcher

### Materials
- Paper plate
- streamers (various colors)
- String
- Markers
- Glue

### Process

**Step 1:** Decorate plate edge like a rainbow

**Step 2:** Attach streamers to bottom of plate

**Step 3:** Add string for hanging

**Result:** A beautiful wind catcher for the classroom!
