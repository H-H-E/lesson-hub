# Grade 1 Science - Sound
## Day 1: Making Sounds

**Duration:** 45 minutes

---

## Learning Objectives

1. **Explore** making different sounds
2. **Identify** sources of sound
3. **Describe** sounds as loud/quiet, high/low

---

## Materials

- [ ] Various instruments (or homemade)
- [ ] Drum, guitar, recorder
- [ ] Sound exploration objects (boxes, rubber bands)
- [ ] Volume scale chart

---

## Opening (5 min)

**Hook:**
"Close your eyes. Listen."

Make various sounds.

"Open your eyes! Where did those sounds come from?"

---

## Exploration (15 min)

**Sound Stations:**

**Station 1: Drums**
- Hit softly/loudly
- What's different?

**Station 2: Rubber Bands**
- Stretch different amounts
- Different pitches?

**Station 3: Voices**
- Sing high/low
- Sing loud/quiet

**Station 4: Bottles**
- Fill with different amounts of water
- Blow across top

---

## Discovery (10 min)

**Chart sounds:**

| Sound | Loud/Quiet | High/Low |
|-------|------------|----------|
| Drum (hit hard) | Loud | Low |
| Drum (hit soft) | Quiet | Low |
| Rubber band (tight) | | High |
| Rubber band (loose) | | Low |

---

## Create (10 min)

**Make an instrument:**

Use materials to create something that makes sound

- Shake it
- Hit it  
- Blow it

---

## Check (5 min)

Exit ticket: "Draw your favorite sound maker"
