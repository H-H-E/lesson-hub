# Grade 3-4 Physical Education: Healthy Active Living - Unit Guide

## Unit Overview

**Duration:** 4 weeks  
**Grades:** 3-4  
**Curriculum Area:** Physical and Health Education

### BC Curriculum Connections
- Daily physical activity promotes healthy body and mind
- Movement skills develop through practice
- Working together and fair play are important

---

## Learning Objectives

Students will be able to:
1. Demonstrate fundamental movement skills
2. Participate in daily physical activity
3. Understand the importance of exercise
4. Work cooperatively with others
5. Follow game rules and demonstrate fair play

---

## Week 1: Active Games and Warm-Ups

### Daily Routine (10 min)

Start every class with:
- 2 minutes jogging in place
- Stretching (reach for sky, touch toes)
- Jumping jacks (10)
- High knees (10)

### Games

**1. Simon Says Fitness**
- "Simon says touch your toes!"
- "Simon says do 5 jumping jacks!"
- Mix of exercises with fitness commands

**2. Animal Walks**
- Bear crawl
- Crab walk
- Frog jump
- Snake slither

**3. Station Rotation**
- 4 stations, 5 min each
- Hula hooping
- Jump rope
- Balancing
- Throwing/catching

---

## Week 2: Ball Skills

### Skills Focus
- Rolling
- Throwing (overhand)
- Catching
- Dribbling

### Activities

**1. Partner Passing**
- Stand 3 feet apart
- Roll ball back and forth (5 min)
- Throw and catch (5 min)

**2. Target Practice**
- Set up cones/buckets
- Roll ball to hit target
- Score points

**3. Relay Races**
- Dribble relay
- Throw and catch relay

---

## Week 3: Cooperative Games

### Focus: Teamwork and Communication

**1. Pass the Ball**
- Everyone in circle
- Pass quickly without dropping
- Beat your time each round

**2. Human Knot**
- Group stands in circle
- Grab hands with two different people
- Untangle without letting go

**3. Trust Walk**
- Partner with eyes closed
- One guides with words only
- Navigate simple obstacle course

---

## Week 4: Dance and Movement

### Creative Movement

**1. Freeze Dance**
- Play music
- Dance freely
- Freeze when music stops!
- Last one moving sits out

**2. Follow the Leader**
- Students take turns being leader
- Leader creates moves
- Everyone copies

**3. Cultural Dances**
- Learn simple dances from different cultures
- Discuss: What movements are easy/challenging?

---

## Assessment

### Observation Checklist

- [ ] Participates in all activities
- [ ] Shows improvement in skills
- [ ] Works well with others
- [ ] Follows rules
- [ ] Shows good sportsmanship

### Skills Rubric

| Skill | Emerging | Developing | Proficient |
|-------|-----------|------------|------------|
| Throwing | Needs support | Sometimes accurate | Controlled throw |
| Catching | Rarely catches | Sometimes catches | Catches consistently |
| Balancing | Loses balance | Holds briefly | Balances well |
| Cooperation | Works alone | Works with help | Leads and supports |

## Safety Reminders

1. Wear proper shoes
2. Drink water before, during, after
3. Warm up before playing
4. Cool down after
5. Tell teacher if hurt or sick
6. Use equipment properly

---

## Differentiation

### For Students Needing Support
- Modified activities
- Partner assistance
- Visual cues

### For Advanced Students
- Lead warm-ups
- Create own games
- Challenge variations
