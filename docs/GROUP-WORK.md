# Group Work Strategies

## Group Formation

### Random
- Popsicle sticks
- Counting off
- Card shuffling

### Purposeful
- Mixed ability
- Learning partners
- Peer tutors

---

## Roles

### Facilitator
- Keeps group on task
- Checks understanding

### Recorder
- Writes down ideas
- Reports to class

### Materials Manager
- Gets supplies
- Returns items

### Time Keeper
- Monitors time
- Signals transitions

---

## Tips
- Short groups (15-20 min)
- Clear expectations
- Practice procedures
- Debrief after
