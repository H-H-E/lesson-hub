# Grade 1 Science - Sky Patterns
## Day 1: The Sun and Moon

**Duration:** 45 minutes

---

## Learning Objectives

1. **Observe** the sun during the day
2. **Notice** the moon at night
3. **Identify** patterns in the sky

---

## Materials

- [ ] Chart: Day Sky / Night Sky
- [ ] Sun and moon images
- [ ] Black and blue paper
- [ ] Yellow and white crayons

---

## Opening (5 min)

**Hook:**
"Raise your hand if you've seen the sun today!"

"Raise your hand if you've seen the moon today!"

"Have you ever seen the sun AND moon in the sky at the same time?"

---

## Exploration (15 min)

**Sky Watch:**

Look out window (or go outside)

**Notice:**
- Where is the sun?
- What color is the sky?
- Any clouds?

**Then show night sky image:**

**Notice:**
- What's in the night sky?
- What color is it?
- What do you see?

---

## Discovery (10 min)

**Create Day/Night Chart:**

| Day Sky | Night Sky |
|---------|-----------|
| Sun | Moon |
| Blue | Black/Dark |
| Clouds | Stars |
| Bright | Twinkling |

---

## Create (10 min)

**Art Activity:**

Paper divided in half:

**Left:** Day sky - draw sun, blue sky, clouds (yellow/blue crayons)

**Right:** Night sky - draw moon, stars, dark sky (white crayons)

---

## Check (5 min)

Share with partner:
"What did you learn about day and night?"
