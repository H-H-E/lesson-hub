# Grade 2 Science - Life Cycles
## Day 1: Life Cycles Everywhere

**Duration:** 45 minutes

---

## Learning Objectives

1. **Identify** that living things have life cycles
2. **Sequence** stages of a life cycle
3. **Compare** different animal life cycles

---

## Materials

- [ ] Butterfly life cycle figures/toys
- [ ] Frog life cycle pictures
- [ ] Chicken life cycle images
- [ ] Sequencing cards (5 sets)
- [ ] Chart paper

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Opening (5 min)

**Hook:**
"Has anyone ever seen a caterpillar?"

"What happened to it?"

"Today we're going to learn about something AMAZING - how animals grow and change!"

---

### Exploration (15 min)

**Activity: Life Cycle Stations**

Three stations:

**Station 1: Butterfly**
- Egg → Caterpillar → Chrysalis → Butterfly
- Students arrange in order

**Station 2: Frog**
- Eggs → Tadpole → Froglet → Frog

**Station 3: Chicken**
- Egg → Chick → Chicken

**Circulate and ask:**
- "What comes first?"
- "How does it change?"
- "What's the same about all of them?"

---

### Discovery (10 min)

**Class discussion:**

"What did we discover?"

**Chart:**

| Stage | Butterfly | Frog | Chicken |
|-------|-----------|------|---------|
| 1 | Egg | Egg | Egg |
| 2 | Caterpillar | Tadpole | Chick |
| 3 | Chrysalis | Froglet | Chicken |
| 4 | Butterfly | Frog | Chicken |

**Key idea:**
ALL living things grow and change!

---

### Create (10 min)

**Sequencing:**

Students glue life cycle in order on paper

- Draw each stage
- Label (with help)
- Arrows between stages

---

### Check (5 min)

Exit ticket: "Name one animal and its life cycle stages"

---

## Assessment

- Sequencing accuracy
- Understanding that all living things grow
- Participation
