## Worksheet 4: Quadrilaterals

| Shape | Sides | Angles |
|-------|-------|--------|
| Square | 4 equal sides | 4 right angles |

| Parallelogram | 2 pairs parallel | Opposite angles equal |
| Rhombus | 4 equal sides | Opposite angles equal |

## Worksheet 7: Perimeter and Area

### Perimeter

**Perimeter = distance around**

```
Example:
   5cm
  ┌─────┐
3cm│     │3cm
  └─────┘
   5cm
P = 5 + 3 + 5 + 3 = 16cm
```

### Find the Perimeter

   P = _______________

2. 5cm × 5cm square

### Area

**Area = space inside**

